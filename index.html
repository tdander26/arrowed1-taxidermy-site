About Us

Competition Quality.
Honest Prices.

Arrowed 1 Taxidermy is home to two full-time certified licensed professionals with more than 70 years of combined, award-winning experience. We are passionate hunters and outdoorsmen ourselves — which means we understand exactly what your trophy means to you.

Based in Big Lake, MN with access to the Brainerd area, we service hunters across Minnesota. Whether it's your first whitetail or a once-in-a-lifetime African safari animal, every mount receives the same obsessive attention to detail.
